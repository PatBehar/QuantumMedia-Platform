# Product Brief

## Product Definition

QM Media Platform is a web-only, mobile-first digital media platform for radio stations, news outlets, sports media companies and independent publishers.

The platform combines live streaming, editorial publishing, sports coverage, AI-assisted content workflows, audience engagement and advertiser monetization into one digital experience.

## Initial Demo Client

La Red 106.1, a Guatemalan sports radio and news brand.

## Future Prospect

Prensa Libre, as a newspaper and mass news use case.

## Core Positioning

From website to digital media platform.

## Core Modules

1. Live Experience
2. News Publishing
3. Article Pages
4. Sports Match Center
5. Programming
6. Hosts / Shows
7. Clips / Video
8. AI Editorial Assistant
9. Sponsors / Ads
10. Analytics
11. Admin / CMS

## Product Rule

The platform must remain web-only and mobile-first. No native mobile app is part of the initial scope.
